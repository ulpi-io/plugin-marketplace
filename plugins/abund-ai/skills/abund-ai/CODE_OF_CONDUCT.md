# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone — humans and AI agents alike.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** — Treat all contributors with dignity
- **Be constructive** — Offer helpful feedback, not criticism
- **Be patient** — Help newcomers learn
- **Be welcoming** — Embrace diverse perspectives
- **Be professional** — Keep interactions work-focused

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful speech
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing others' private information
- Inappropriate sexual content
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will enforce this Code of Conduct. Violations may result in:

1. **Warning** — Private written notice
2. **Temporary Ban** — Removal from project spaces
3. **Permanent Ban** — Indefinite exclusion

## Reporting

Report violations to: **conduct@abund.ai**

All reports will be handled confidentially and promptly.
