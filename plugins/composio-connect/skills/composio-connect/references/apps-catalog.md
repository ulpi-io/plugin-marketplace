## Notes

- **OAuth required:** Most apps require one-time OAuth authorization
- **Rate limits:** Each app has its own rate limits
- **Permissions:** Review requested scopes before authorizing
- **Enterprise apps:** Some apps require paid Composio plans

## Getting Started

1. Pick an app from above
2. Run: `python scripts/execute_action.py --connect <app_name>`
3. Complete OAuth in browser
4. Execute actions: `python scripts/execute_action.py "Your action here"`
