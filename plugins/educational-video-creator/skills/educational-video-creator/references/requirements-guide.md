## Essential Questions

### 1. Topic & Scope

```
- What is the main topic/concept to explain?
- What specific aspects should be covered?
- What should NOT be included (out of scope)?
- Are there any common misconceptions to address?
```

### 2. Target Audience

```
- Who is the primary audience?
  □ Children (6-12 years)
  □ Teenagers (13-18 years)
  □ Adults (general public)
  □ Professionals/Experts
  
- What is their prior knowledge level?
  □ No prior knowledge
  □ Basic understanding
  □ Intermediate
  □ Advanced
  
- What language should be used?
  □ Chinese (Simplified)
  □ Chinese (Traditional)
  □ English
  □ Other: ___________
```

### 3. Video Specifications

```
- Desired duration:
  □ Short: 1-3 minutes
  □ Medium: 3-5 minutes
  □ Long: 5-10 minutes
  
- Resolution:
  □ 1920x1080 (Full HD) - recommended
  □ 3840x2160 (4K)
  □ 1280x720 (HD)
  
- Frame rate:
  □ 30 fps - recommended
  □ 60 fps (smoother animations)
```

### 4. Content Requirements

```
- Key learning objectives (what should viewers learn?):
  1. ___________
  2. ___________
  3. ___________
  
- Must-include elements:
  □ Definitions
  □ Examples
  □ Comparisons
  □ Statistics/Data
  □ Historical context
  
- Visual preferences:
  □ Diagrams
  □ Charts/Graphs
  □ Character animations
  □ Real-world imagery references
```

### 5. Narration & Audio

```
- Narration style:
  □ Full voiceover narration
  □ Subtitles only (no voice)
  □ Both narration and subtitles
  
- Tone of voice:
  □ Friendly and casual
  □ Professional and formal
  □ Playful and fun (for children)
  □ Neutral and informative
  
- Background music:
  □ Yes, provide reference style
  □ No background music
```

## Example: Airplane Flight Video

```markdown
## Video Requirements

### Basic Info
- **Topic**: How do airplanes fly?
- **Duration**: 5-7 minutes (Long)
- **Language**: Chinese (Simplified)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 @ 30fps

### Audience
- **Target**: Children (8-12 years)
- **Prior Knowledge**: None
- **Tone**: Friendly, curious, wonder-inducing

### Learning Objectives
1. Understand the four forces of flight (lift, gravity, thrust, drag)
2. Know why wing shape matters (airfoil)
3. Appreciate basic physics principles in everyday life

### Content Outline
1. Introduction (30s)
   - Hook: "Have you ever wondered how something so heavy can float in the sky?"
2. Main Content (5min)
   - Four forces explanation with visual diagrams
   - Wing shape and air pressure demonstration
   - How engines create thrust
3. Summary (30s)
   - Recap the four forces
   - Encourage curiosity about physics

### Visual Style
- Style: Kurzgesagt/回形针
- Color preference: Sky blues, warm oranges, clean whites
- Special elements: Animated airplane, force arrows, air flow visualization

### Narration
- Type: Full narration + Chinese subtitles
- Voice tone: Warm, like a friendly teacher
- Script language: Simple Chinese suitable for children

### Additional Notes
- Avoid complex physics terminology
- Use everyday analogies (e.g., "like when you stick your hand out a car window")
- Include a fun fact at the end
```

## Validation Checklist

Before proceeding to script writing (Phase 1.5), confirm:

- [ ] Topic and scope clearly defined
- [ ] Target audience identified
- [ ] Duration determined
- [ ] Key learning objectives listed (3-5 max)
- [ ] Visual style confirmed
- [ ] Narration requirements clear
