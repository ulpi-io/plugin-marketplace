# Weather-Based Clothing Guidance

## Hot Weather (≥ 30°C)
- Lightweight, breathable fabrics (cotton, linen, moisture-wicking)
- Light colors, wide-brimmed hat, sunglasses, SPF 30+
- Breathable sneakers or sandals; reusable water bottle
- If ≥40°C: minimize midday exposure, carry electrolytes, UPF clothing

## Mild Weather (10–30°C)
- Layers: t-shirt + light jacket or cardigan
- Long pants or jeans; closed-toe shoes or sneakers
- Light scarf for cooler ends of range

## Cold Weather (≤ 10°C)
- Warm base layer (thermal for sub-zero)
- Insulating mid-layer (fleece, wool)
- Windproof/waterproof outer layer (parka, down jacket)
- Insulated waterproof boots, warm hat, gloves, scarf
- If ≤0°C: heavy parka, hand warmers, double socks

## Rainy Weather (precip prob ≥ 50% or >1mm)
- Waterproof jacket with hood
- Water-resistant pants or quick-dry fabrics
- Compact umbrella, waterproof shoes
- Waterproof bag cover, extra dry socks

## Windy Weather (≥ 30 km/h)
- Windproof outer layer (windbreaker, hardshell)
- Snug-fitting clothes, secured headwear
- Sunglasses for dust, lip balm
- Wind chill: layer up more than temperature alone suggests

## Combinations
| Conditions | Advice |
|---|---|
| Hot + Windy | Light windbreaker over breathable clothes |
| Cold + Rainy | Waterproof insulated jacket, waterproof boots |
| Cold + Windy | Windproof insulated layer, protect face/extremities |
| Mild + Rainy | Light rain jacket, umbrella |
| Hot + Rainy | Quick-dry fabrics, watch for slippery surfaces |
