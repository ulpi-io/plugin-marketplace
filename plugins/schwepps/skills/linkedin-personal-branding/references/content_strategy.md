## Content Pillar Framework

Define 3-5 content pillars that represent expertise areas and audience interests.

### Pillar Structure

| Pillar Type | Description | Example Topics |
|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| **Expertise** | Core professional knowledge | Industry insights, how-to guides, tips |
| **Experience** | Personal stories and lessons | Career journey, failures, wins |
| **Opinion** | Thought leadership, takes | Industry trends, predictions, debates |
| **Curation** | Valuable external content | Industry news, research, tools |
| **Personal** | Humanizing content | Behind-the-scenes, values, interests |

### Pillar Ratio Recommendation

```
40% Expertise (educational value)
25% Experience (relatability)
20% Opinion (thought leadership)
10% Curation (industry awareness)
5% Personal (authenticity)
```

### Example Content Pillars

**For a Product Manager:**
1. Product strategy and roadmapping
2. User research and data analysis
3. Career growth and leadership
4. Tech industry trends
5. Personal productivity

**For a Marketing Professional:**
1. Content marketing tactics
2. Data-driven marketing
3. Brand building
4. Marketing tools and tech
5. Professional growth

## Posting Schedule

### Optimal Posting Frequency

| Level | Posts/Week | Best For |
|-------|------------|----------|
| Minimum | 3 | Maintaining visibility |
| Optimal | 5-7 | Building momentum |
| Maximum | 10-14 | Creator mode, aggressive growth |

### Best Posting Times (General Guidelines)

| Day | Best Times (local) | Notes |
|-----|-------------------|-------|
| Tuesday | 7-8 AM, 10-11 AM, 5-6 PM | Highest overall engagement |
| Wednesday | 7-8 AM, 10-11 AM, 12 PM | Strong mid-week performance |
| Thursday | 7-8 AM, 10-11 AM, 5-6 PM | Good engagement before weekend |
| Monday | 10-11 AM, 12 PM | People catching up after weekend |
| Friday | 9-10 AM | Lower engagement, test with light content |
| Weekend | Avoid unless testing | Generally lower engagement |

### Testing Strategy

1. Post at different times for 2-3 weeks
2. Track impressions and engagement by time slot
3. Identify your audience's active times
4. Double down on best-performing slots

## Content Formats

### Format Performance Ranking

| Format | Engagement | Effort | Best Use Cases |
|--------|------------|--------|----------------|
| Carousel/Document | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | High | Guides, lists, frameworks |
| Native Video | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | High | Stories, tutorials, personality |
| Text + Image | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Medium | Tips, insights, announcements |
| Text Only | ⭐⭐⭐ | Low | Stories, opinions, quick thoughts |
| Poll | ⭐⭐⭐ | Low | Research, engagement, conversation |
| Link Post | ⭐⭐ | Low | Sharing external content |
| Article | ⭐⭐ | High | Long-form thought leadership |
| Newsletter | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | High | Building subscriber base |

### Carousel Best Practices

- **Slide count:** 8-12 slides optimal
- **First slide:** Attention-grabbing hook/title
- **Design:** Consistent branding, readable fonts
- **Content:** One idea per slide
- **Last slide:** CTA (follow, comment, save)
- **File format:** PDF for document uploads

### Video Best Practices

- **Length:** 30-90 seconds ideal
- **First 3 seconds:** Hook immediately
- **Captions:** Always include (85% watch without sound)
- **Orientation:** Square or vertical for mobile
- **Quality:** Good lighting, clear audio
- **CTA:** Include verbal and text CTA

### Text Post Best Practices

- **Length:** 1,200-1,500 characters
- **Hook:** First 2-3 lines crucial (before "see more")
- **Formatting:** Short paragraphs, line breaks
- **Structure:** Hook → Story → Lesson → CTA
- **Emojis:** Use sparingly for visual breaks

## Writing Formulas

### The Hook Formula (First 2-3 Lines)

**Pattern 1: Controversial Statement**
```
[Unpopular opinion/Bold claim]
Here's why...
```

**Pattern 2: Story Hook**
```
[Time reference] I [surprising event]
[Unexpected outcome]
```

**Pattern 3: Curiosity Gap**
```
The #1 mistake [audience] makes with [topic]:
(And how to fix it)
```

**Pattern 4: Social Proof**
```
I've [impressive achievement] in [timeframe].
Here are the [number] things that made the difference:
```

**Pattern 5: Question**
```
What would you do if [scenario]?
I faced this situation last week...
```

### Post Structure Templates

**The Story Framework (STAR)**
```
Situation: Set the scene
Task: What needed to be done
Action: What you did
Result: The outcome + lesson
```

**The List Framework**
```
Hook: [Number] [things/tips/lessons] about [topic]

1. [Point 1]
   └ Brief explanation

2. [Point 2]
   └ Brief explanation

[Continue...]

CTA: What's your #[number]?
```

**The Opinion Framework**
```
Hot take: [Controversial statement]

Here's why:

[Point 1]
[Point 2]  
[Point 3]

Agree or disagree? Let me know below 👇
```

### CTA Examples

- "What's your experience with this?"
- "Drop a 🔥 if you agree"
- "Save this for later 📌"
- "Share with someone who needs this"
- "Follow for more [topic] insights"
- "What would you add to this list?"
- "Tag a [role] who should see this"

## Hashtag Strategy

### Hashtag Best Practices

| Factor | Recommendation |
|--------|----------------|
| Quantity | 3-5 hashtags per post |
| Placement | End of post or first comment |
| Mix | 1 broad + 2-3 niche + 1 branded |

### Hashtag Categories

**Broad (1M+ followers):** High visibility, high competition
- #leadership #marketing #technology #business #entrepreneurship

**Medium (100K-1M):** Good balance of reach and targeting
- #productmanagement #saas #startup #careeradvice

**Niche (<100K):** Targeted audience, lower competition
- #techleadership #b2bmarketing #remotework

**Branded:** Build your own presence
- Create a unique hashtag for your content series

### Research Process

1. Search hashtags in LinkedIn search
2. Check follower count
3. Review recent posts for engagement
4. Test and track which drive impressions

## Engagement Tactics

### Comment Strategy

**Rule of 15:**
- Comment on 15 posts daily
- 5 minutes before posting
- 10 minutes after posting
- Focus on posts from target audience and industry leaders

**Quality Comment Framework:**
1. Add value (insight, experience, question)
2. 3+ sentences minimum
3. Reference specific points from the post
4. Ask thoughtful questions

**Bad Comment:** "Great post! 👏"
**Good Comment:** "This resonates deeply. When I implemented [related experience], I found that [specific insight]. Curious - have you seen [related question]?"

### Engagement Timing

| Action | When | Why |
|--------|------|-----|
| Comment on others | Before your post | Algorithm boost |
| Reply to comments | Within 1-2 hours | Signals engagement |
| Like comments | Immediately | Encourages more comments |
| Tag in posts | Sparingly | When genuinely relevant |

### Building Relationships

1. **Identify 50-100 key connections** in your industry
2. **Engage consistently** on their content
3. **Add value** through comments, not just likes
4. **Send occasional DMs** with genuine observations
5. **Collaborate** on content when appropriate

## Measurement & Iteration

### Weekly Review Questions

1. Which posts performed best? Why?
2. What topics resonated most?
3. What formats drove engagement?
4. What time slots worked best?
5. What will I do differently next week?

### Content Optimization Process

1. **Post** with hypothesis about what will work
2. **Measure** performance after 48 hours
3. **Analyze** what drove engagement
4. **Learn** patterns and preferences
5. **Iterate** based on insights
6. **Repeat** with new experiments
