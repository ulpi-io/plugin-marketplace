# Viral Hooks - Validations

## Hook Doesn't Match Content

### **Id**
hook-content-gap
### **Severity**
high
### **Type**
conceptual
### **Check**
Hook should accurately represent content
### **Indicators**
  - Promised vs delivered mismatch
  - High bounce rate
### **Message**
Hook promises more than content delivers.

Align hook with actual content value
